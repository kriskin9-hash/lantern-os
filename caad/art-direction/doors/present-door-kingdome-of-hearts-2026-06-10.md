The Present Door shows the world outside as it is today: the good, the bad, and the ugly seen in a new light.

This door belongs to the **Kingdome of Hearts** and the **Three Doors** game frame. It should not be treated as the old false anchors such as Signal Tower or Moving Room. It is a living present-tense door: the world as it stands now, made visible through three concerns Alex may care about.
