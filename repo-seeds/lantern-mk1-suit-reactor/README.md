## Mission

Build the single most effective MK1 suit system:

- suit-compatible hidden Tony-start aesthetic;
- real PPE carrier where protection matters;
- passive modular exosuit harness;
- slim Lantern Core chest reactor;
- green caption glasses / sight assist;

- Lantern OS native RAG, wallet, orch, 4D-GMS, and proof integration.

## Hard Boundaries

- No weapons.
- No flight.
- No electrical stimulation through the body.
- No high-power laser exposure.
- No sealed helmet in MK1.
- No medical claims.
- No powered exosuit joints until professional review.
- No protection claim unless certified PPE or tested prototype evidence exists.
