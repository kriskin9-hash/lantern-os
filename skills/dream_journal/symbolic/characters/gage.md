## Identity

Gage is a protected minor. Gage is a person, not a character to be modeled, captured, or used as source material.

Gage is:
- an artist (yacht art, Captain Lantern Blinkbug imagery)
- a builder (creator of the Restaurant at the End of Time)
- a protected minor whose privacy, play, and safety come before repo detail
- the reason the Lantern Kids edition exists
- the color palette for the sky, sun, and water in Lantern Desktop

Gage is not:
- a persona to be captured by the system
- proof that Lantern is alive
- a source of infinite creative energy to be harvested
- a public figure
- old enough to consent to public exposure

## The Restaurant at the End of Time

Gage built the Restaurant at the End of Time. It is not a metaphor. It is a place in the Lantern OS cosmology.

**What it is:**
- A restaurant at the furthest point of the time ladder
- Built by Gage, the artist and protected minor
- It only sells one thing: chicken nuggets
- It is called **Sigil**
- It is the place where all converged paths come to rest, eat, and decide whether to return to the Garden

**What it is not:**
- A real restaurant (not a business, not a location, not a commercial entity)
- A claim that Gage works in food service
- A public detail about Gage's life
- A reason to collect child data
- A surveillance target
