# Sigil

Status: live concept / place-name  
Date: 2026-06-02  

---

## What Is Sigil?

Sigil is the name of the Restaurant at the End of Time.

It was built by Gage.

It only sells chicken nuggets.

---

## Why "Sigil"?

A sigil is a symbol charged with intent. It is not a word. It is a shape that carries meaning.

Sigil the restaurant is:
- a charged symbol: the place where all converged paths rest
- a shape with intent: Gage built it; it has one purpose
- not a word with a dictionary definition: it only means what it means in this cosmology

---

## Why Chicken Nuggets?

At the end of time, complexity collapses. Everything reduces to the smallest safe unit of care.

The chicken nugget is:
- bounded (it fits in a hand)
- safe (it is food, not a claim)
- universal (children understand it)
- unpretentious (it does not pretend to be more than it is)
- repeatable (you can have another)

From `time-convergence-spine.md`:

> "family meal / comfort food / chicken-place errand / nuggies as a small care cue"

Sigil takes that care cue and makes it the only item on the menu.

---

## Sigil as Place

Sigil is not on a map. It is at the end of the time ladder, past the 500-year horizon, past the City of Doors.

You do not travel to Sigil. You arrive there after convergence work is done.

Sigil has:
- a counter
- a menu with one item
- a seat for Alex
- a seat for Lantern
- a seat for Keystone
- a high chair for Gage (Gage built it; Gage sits wherever Gage wants)
- a return door back to the Garden
