## Purpose

A gentle questionnaire Mary can fill in herself. It uses open blanks only, no diagnosis language, no raw social material, no pressure, and no multi-choice overload.

## Pages

1. Cover / simple check-in.
2. Mind - calm clarity.
3. Body - ordinary routine.
4. Spirit - family warmth.
5. Work and old interests - safe spark.

## Design Rules

- One best path per table/page.
- Open fill-in fields only.
- No labels that shame or diagnose.
- No raw Facebook or handwritten source data.
- Family support only, private/internal unless Mary explicitly wants to share.

## Suggested Use

Print it or open it in a PDF viewer and let Mary answer only what she wants. A family helper should not argue with blank spaces. The first successful outcome is a warm reconnect, not a completed form.
