## Capital Allocation Rules

### 70% to Proven Channels

**Definition:** Channels with >80% confidence of converting to revenue

**Example allocation:** 
- Institutional partnership outreach: 40% of budget
- Customer onboarding support: 20%
- Institutional pilot execution: 10%

**When to use:** After first 2-3 successful pilots, commit 70% to replicating success

### 20% to Emerging Opportunities

**Definition:** Opportunities with 40-80% confidence, showing promise

**Example allocation:**
- Direct-to-consumer outreach: 10%
- Partner integrations (pharmacy, wearable): 10%

**When to use:** Test adjacent channels while main channel scales

### 10% to Long-Shot Bets

**Definition:** Experimental approaches, <40% confidence

**Example allocation:**
- Employer health benefit partnerships: 5%
- Clinical trial recruitment partnerships: 5%

**When to use:** Maintain optionality without destabilizing core business

---

## Reallocation Trigger Rules

### Automatic Reallocation When:

1. **Proven channel drops below 80% confidence:**
   - → Shift 10% back to proven category
   - → Investigate what changed
   - → Plan fix or pivot

2. **Emerging opportunity hits 80% confidence:**
   - → Promote to proven channel status
   - → Reallocate 20% to this channel
   - → Move prior emerging opp to long-shot

3. **Long-shot bet shows early traction (>30% of expected):**
   - → Run small validation experiment
   - → If successful, promote to emerging
   - → Allocate 5% more budget

4. **Monthly review (first Friday of month):**
   - → Review all channel confidence scores
   - → Check allocation vs. reality
   - → Rebalance if >10% variance

## Monthly Financial Synthesis

### First Friday of Month (1 hour)

**Review:**
1. **Cash position:** Actual burn vs. target
2. **Revenue:** Actual vs. target by channel
3. **Runway:** Months remaining at current rate
4. **Trend:** Improving? Stable? Declining?

**Decisions:**
1. **Adjust burn if needed:** Cut costs or reduce scope?
2. **Accelerate revenue if possible:** Extra pilot outreach?
3. **Allocation changes:** Based on channel performance
4. **Runway status:** Still 9+ months? Adjust timeline?

**Outputs:**
- Monthly P&L summary
- Runway projection (updated)
- Next month budget
- Decision log

## Red Flags - When to Adjust Immediately

🚨 **Runway below 6 months:** Consider raising capital or cutting burn by 50%

🚨 **Revenue declining month-to-month:** Investigate channel issues immediately

🚨 **Burn exceeding $500/week:** Cut discretionary spend that week

🚨 **Institutional pilots not converting:** Pivot messaging or target by month 2

🚨 **No revenue by end of Month 2:** Extend runway or reduce scope
