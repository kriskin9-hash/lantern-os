This page is used as an analogy for Lantern OS governance:

- protocol-level changes need process;
- no single actor should silently force high-risk changes;
- proposal, review, iteration, testing, and broad support matter;
- forks/splits are a real risk when values or technical decisions diverge;
- offchain governance can be legitimate when decisions are public and
  evidence-backed.
