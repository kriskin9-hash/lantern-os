      What the view is, and what it is not

      This landscape reads your local threads only. It shows patterns, not prophecies. It remembers presence, not capability. It does not diagnose, predict, or claim to know you better than you know yourself.

      Pretty words stay pretty here. The view holds shape without making it proof, pressure, permission, or command.
