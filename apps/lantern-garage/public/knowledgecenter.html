Looped latent reasoning (arXiv 2510.25741) — adaptive depth + Q-exit CDF, implemented over our local model (loop-reasoner.js) and grounded in the KB.

Convergence-exit spiral ?Instead of stopping when the model is "confident enough" (Q-exit), keep looping until its internal state stops moving — a true fixed point. Falsifiable, with a shipped prototype.

Recasts Ouro's Q-exit as convergence-exit (loop until ‖Δh‖/‖h‖<ε), then loop→spiral→tesseract over the Status-Cube. External Reality Rule: every claim tagged implemented / proven-conditional / hypothesis, with 4 falsifiable experiments.

Convergence Core spine ?The design for turning Lantern's already-built parts (memory, task, tool, record + the Σ₀ safety system) into one closed agent loop — and the single feedback wire that's still missing.

The four core objects, the six-stage loop, and the write-back closure already exist in code (kernel.py, verify.py); the gap is that none of it runs end-to-end in the serving path. Verified file-by-file, with the honest "durable agent, not AGI" ceiling.
