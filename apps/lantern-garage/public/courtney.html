    What this is, and what it is not

    This is a symbolic sandbox for private reflection. It stores your words on this machine only. It does not claim to heal, diagnose, predict, or remember better than you do. It does not sync to clouds or post anywhere. Discord commands keep only the speaker's own threads.

    Pretty words stay pretty here. The well holds meaning without making it proof, pressure, permission, or command.
