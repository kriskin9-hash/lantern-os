What am I looking at?

This is an AI trading assistant. It watches a list of stocks, looks for good moments to buy or sell, and shows its thinking here. Right now it's in practice mode — everything is pretend, so no real money is ever at risk.
PAPER (top-left badge)
Practice mode. Trades are simulated in a safe sandbox — nothing touches a real account.
Equity
The total pretend value of the account right now: cash plus whatever the holdings are currently worth.
Day P&L
Profit or Loss so far today. Green means up, red means down.
VIX
The market's "fear gauge." Higher numbers mean a more nervous, jumpy market; lower numbers mean calm.
The stock cards
Each card is one stock the assistant is watching. ▲ LONG = it expects the price to rise; ▼ SHORT = it expects a fall. The percentage is how confident it is.
The colored ladder on each card
Price levels that matter. Green zones (support) are where a stock tends to stop falling; red zones (resistance) are where it tends to stop rising. The bright line is the current price.
Agent Feed
Live notes from the AI as it thinks and acts — like reading its train of thought.
Kalshi Odds
Live odds from a real prediction market (e.g. sports games) that the system also tracks as a signal. "Terminal →" opens the full betting screen.
Tip: hover over any number in the top bar to see what it means.
