The Problem

Most AI tools are black boxes in the cloud. You feed them data, get output, and lose the thread. There is no evidence trail, no local memory, no way to verify what changed or why.

Lantern OS keeps the loop local. Every claim is sourced. Every action is logged. Every product is evidence-weighted.

The Product

  • Local RAG Dollhouse — Flat-file knowledge base with SHA256 manifests. No vector DB required.
  • Arc Reactor Confidence — Brier-style scoring. No fake certainty. Claims rise only with evidence.
  • Convergence Loop — 12-step inspect-before-edit workflow. Fix the first 2-4 issues before expansion.
  • Agent Fleet — Design-contract ring slots with elastic pool targets. Not live worker spam.
  • Dreamer Journal — Private local notebook for sparks, dreams, and half-remembered truths.
  • Payment Bridge — Stripe-integrated wallet ledger. Every dollar traced from invoice to cleared cash.
