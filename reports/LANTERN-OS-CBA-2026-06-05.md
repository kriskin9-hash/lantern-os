## 7. Limitations & Qualifications

1. **Revenue is speculative.** No paying customers exist yet. The $1,000 founding seat pool is a test, not a validated market.
2. **Efficiency gains assume hourly value.** The $75/hr rate reflects founder time; actual market rate for this skillset is higher.
3. **Intangible value is unvalidated.** IP and option value use rough estimates.
4. **Movie 2/3 are high-variance.** Confidence drops to 61% and 29% respectively.
5. **No discounting applied.** A 10% discount rate would reduce long-horizon NPV but not materially affect Movie 1.
