## Executive Summary

This report tracks the convergence of the Mind Body Spirit Shine video content pipeline. It establishes the Publication Gate criteria that must be satisfied before any video is published.

## Status: validated candidate

The pipeline is functional but gated. The three-flashes-per-second safety rule and caption requirements must be verified before promotion.

## Publication Gate

Before any video in this pipeline is published publicly, the following must be confirmed:

1. **Captions** — all video content includes accurate, synchronized captions
2. **Seizure safety** — no sequence contains three-flashes-per-second or faster flashing
3. **Privacy** — no request for comments, names, photos, location, account signup, or personal data is embedded in or linked from the video
4. **Rollback** — a rollback procedure is documented and tested before any public release

## Content Constraints

- No claims of public-release readiness without gate sign-off
- No promotion of status from candidate to production without operator approval
- All videos reviewed against the three-flashes-per-second threshold
- Captions are required on all published content

## Rollback

If a published video needs to be retracted: remove from hosting, document the retraction in this file, and mark status as retracted.
