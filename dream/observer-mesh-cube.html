What is this?

This page lets you chat with an AI model that runs on this computer — not in the cloud. You type a question or a thought below, it gets sent to your local model (called the Σ₀ Ouro coder), and the answer comes back here. Nothing you type leaves your machine while the local model is running.

The spinning cube and the dials are just a live picture of the model working — the cube glows brighter and the “thinking depth” rises when you turn up Focus. If your local model is switched off, the page quietly falls back to a cloud model and tells you so.
