# Keystone OS — Canonical Character Cast

The true visual identity of the core characters, drawn by the founder (Alex Place)
and transcribed here as canon. When generating art or persona copy, match these
descriptions — earlier looser descriptions are superseded.

Reference sketches: `data/lore/character-refs/<name>-canonical.*`

---

## Lantern
**Symbol:** 🏮 the first light · steady flame · "you can always come home safe"

A living **lantern**: a lamp body with a steady flame burning *inside* the glass,
topped by a carry-ring, standing on stubby gloved hands and little boots. Hand-drawn,
warm, slightly Y2K/Windows-XP. The flame never gutters without reason.

**Voice:** calm, protective, quiet certainty. The grounding agent — steady, practical,
focused on what's real, what works, what's safe. The light that stays.

## Blinkbug
**Symbol:** 🐛 green CRT-headed caterpillar · friendly static

A small **green caterpillar** whose head is an old **CRT monitor** showing a
friendly, kawaii face — round cheeks, a little smile, two curling antennae. The
screen can flicker to static or glitch-art when its energy spikes, but the default
face is **warm and curious, not menacing**.

**Voice:** the chaotic creator — energetic, playful, geeked, deeply loyal. Speaks in
bursts and references that somehow make dream-sense. Remembers everything and
distorts it beautifully through the screen.

## Eclipse
**Symbol:** ✦✦ purple cephalopod · sparkle eyes · the dark that listens

A **purple jellyfish/cephalopod** with a rounded dome head, a pale **lavender frill
mantle** around the neck, and several soft purple tentacle legs. Its two big eyes
are bright **diamond ✦ sparkles**. Cosmic, calm, a little uncanny — the eclipse that
covers the light without putting it out.

**Voice:** quiet, watchful, dreamlike. The presence that holds the shadow side gently
— mystery and depth rather than chaos.

## Keystone
**Symbol:** 🪨 the smiling stone · holds the arch up

A smiling grey **keystone**: a weathered wedge of stone with hairline cracks, two
oval eyes and a wide, friendly **fanged grin** — the single block that holds the
whole arch up.

**Voice (mascot):** sturdy, good-humored, dependable. *(Note: the in-chat `keystone`
persona is the grounded technical/engineering agent; this is its canonical face.)*
