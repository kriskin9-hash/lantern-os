What Dream Journal Does

Dream Journal is a private, local-first system for capturing, analyzing, and understanding your dreams. Everything runs on your machine. Nothing is sent anywhere without your permission.

Three ways to use it:

  • Web surface: Open surfaces/dream-journal/index.html in any browser. No server. Data stays in browser localStorage.
  • Discord: Type /dream or !dream in any channel where Lantern bot is present.
  • Garage dashboard: Full analytics at http://127.0.0.1:4177.

Quick Start (5 minutes)

1. Open Dream Journal

Choose the surface that fits your workflow. The web surface works immediately with no setup.

2. Create Your First Entry

Every entry has:

  • Dream text — What you remember
  • Lucidity — 0 to 10, how aware you were
  • Emotions — awe, fear, joy, curiosity, sadness, love
  • Tags — Keywords for search and patterns
  • Linked goals — Connect dreams to life direction

3. Review & Reflect

Use the Generate Mirror Prompt button to copy an AI-ready prompt that analyzes your recent dreams for patterns, symbols, and waking-life connections.

Entry Types

Understanding the Scales

Lucidity (0 to 10)

ScoreMeaning
0Completely non-lucid. Dream feels real.
3Slight awareness. Something seems odd.
5Partial lucidity. Know you're dreaming, limited control.
7High lucidity. Can control movement, speech, some environment.
10Full lucidity. Complete awareness and control.

Emotions

Tag what you actually felt. Dreams are complex — tag both wonder and fear if both were present.

Tags

Be consistent. Use flying always, not sometimes flight. Consistency makes the tag cloud and search powerful.

Best Practices

Daily Recording

Best time: Within 5 minutes of waking. Write 2-5 sentences. Fragments count:

Falling. Water. Felt safe.

Weekly Review (15 min)

  1. Look at recent entries
  2. Notice emotions and tags
  3. Create one mirror entry with an insight

Monthly Deep Dive (30 min)

  1. Identify 2-3 recurring symbols
  2. Check lucidity trends
  3. Record findings in a lore or mirror entry

Privacy & Storage

Everything stays local.

  • Web surface: Data in browser localStorage. Export as JSONL anytime.
  • Discord: Data saved to data/dreamer/notebooks/<user>.jsonl on the host machine.
  • Structured skill: data/dream_journal/dreams_YYYY-MM.jsonl

Backup: Copy the data/dream_journal/ folder. That's it.

No deletion: Entries are append-only. Your original memories are part of your history.
