# Safety Boundary

Status: active sandbox boundary.

## Allowed

- metaphor, poetry, names, images, maps, and story fragments;
- emotional meaning stated as personal interpretation;
- speculative worldbuilding clearly labeled as speculative;
- private notes that avoid identifying or pressuring other people;
- gentle review prompts that return the operator to food, rest, care, consent, and reality checks.

## Blocked

- treating a symbol as proof;
- treating a symbol as consent;
- treating a symbol as diagnosis, cure, prophecy, destiny, command, or emergency plan;
- using a real person as a symbol without current consent;
- posting private family, health, location, schedule, or relationship details;
- instructions for surveillance, coercion, self-harm, harm to others, or bypassing consent;
- runtime agent authority, hidden automation, repo sync, deployment, or public release claims.

## Quarantine Triggers

Move material into `quarantine/` when it includes:

- urgency that says "act now" without evidence;
- grand certainty after little sleep or high stress;
- private-person names or identifying detail;
- medical, legal, financial, spiritual, or emergency implications;
- pressure to contact someone;
- claims that a dream, sign, or symbol proves reality;
- language that feels too powerful to review calmly.

## Review Questions

```text
What is beautiful here?
What is true only as feeling or symbol?
What would be harmful if literalized?
Who could be pressured or exposed?
What boring care action returns this to life?
What, if anything, is safe to promote?
```

## Emergency Boundary

This repo is not crisis care. If there is immediate danger, inability to stay safe, severe confusion, or risk of harm to self or others, use qualified human support and emergency resources instead of repo work.
