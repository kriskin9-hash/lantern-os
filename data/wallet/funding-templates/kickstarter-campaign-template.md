## Elevator Pitch
Lantern OS is an open-source operating system that revolutionizes how businesses deploy and manage AI agents. Run 36+ specialized agents in a coordinated environment with built-in payment processing, task orchestration, and local-first privacy.

## The Story
AI agents are transforming business operations, but managing multiple AI systems is fragmented, expensive, and complex. Lantern OS solves this by providing a unified, open-source platform for orchestrating AI agents with enterprise-grade features at startup-friendly costs.
