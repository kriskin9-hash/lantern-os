## Partner Identification (Week 1)

### Target Profile
- **Healthcare setting:** Primary care clinics, family medicine practices, rural health centers
- **Patient volume:** 50-500 patient families
- **Tech adoption:** Moderate to high (EMR in use, willing to integrate new tools)
- **Pain points:** Care coordination gaps, family engagement, chronic disease management
- **Decision maker:** Medical director, practice manager, or clinic administrator

### Priority List (5-10 institutions)
1. Rural family medicine clinics (underserved communities)
2. Geriatric care practices (older adult focus)
3. Pediatric multi-specialty groups (family coordination)
4. Community health centers (low-income patient base)
5. Mental health + primary care integration programs

### Research Method
- LinkedIn search: "clinic director" + location + keywords
- Google Maps: "family medicine" + "community health" + target area
- Professional networks: State medical societies, health information exchanges
- Referrals: Reach out to existing advisors/contacts

## Outreach Messaging (Week 1-2)

### Privacy-First Positioning
**Subject Line:** "Free family care coordination tool for your practice"

**Email Body:**
```
Hi [Name],

I'm building COMET LEAPER—a privacy-first family care coordination 
platform designed for healthcare practices like yours.

Problem: Family care is fragmented. Older adults, patients with chronic 
diseases, and families managing multiple conditions lack a simple way to 
coordinate care across providers.

Solution: We provide secure, HIPAA-compliant care coordination that:
- Gives families one place to organize medical information
- Reduces duplicate tests and medication errors
- Improves patient engagement and health outcomes
- Integrates with your existing EMR

Ask: Would you be interested in a 4-week pilot with 10-20 patient families 
from your practice? (Free. No cost, no obligation.)

Let me know if this resonates.

Best,
[Founder Name]
```

### Value Props to Test
1. **For clinics:** Reduced no-shows, better medication adherence, lower readmission rates
2. **For families:** One place for all medical info, better communication with providers
3. **For health outcomes:** Coordinated preventive care, early intervention, patient empowerment

### Success Metrics
- Email open rate: >30%
- Response rate: >10% (at least 1-2 positive responses per 10 emails)
- Meeting conversion: >50% (of responses → scheduled calls)
- Pilot interest: >30% (of calls → willing to pilot)

## Onboarding Workflow (Week 2-3)

### Day 1: Kickoff Call
- **Participants:** Medical director/practice manager + founder
- **Duration:** 30 minutes
- **Agenda:**
  - Overview of platform, privacy/security
  - Select 10-20 patient families (criteria: engaged, diverse diagnoses)
  - Identify clinic champion (point person for questions)
  - Set Week 4 review date

### Day 2-3: Family Onboarding
- **Method:** Email + phone (personalized outreach from clinic)
- **Message:** "Your doctor is trying a new family care tool. Free trial, 4 weeks."
- **Sign-up:** Self-guided online, includes email verification
- **Setup:** Profile creation, family member access, medication entry

### Week 1: Weekly Check-in
- **Call:** Founder + clinic champion
- **Topics:** Adoption rate, technical issues, early feedback
- **Action:** Address blockers, encourage weekly usage

### Week 2-3: Usage Monitoring
- **Dashboard:** Track active users, features used, engagement patterns
- **Support:** Email/phone support for families and clinic staff
- **Feedback:** Weekly survey (usage, usefulness, issues)

### Week 4: Final Review & Decision
- **Meeting:** Clinic leadership + founder
- **Data presented:**
  - Adoption rate (% of families active)
  - Engagement (avg logins/week, features used)
  - Patient feedback (satisfaction, value)
  - Clinic feedback (integration, support, usability)
  - Clinical outcomes (if measurable in 4 weeks)

**Decision options:**
1. **Continue:** 12-week paid partnership (revenue split)
2. **Extend trial:** 4 more weeks (validate metrics further)
3. **Discontinue:** Partner decline (collect exit feedback)

## Revenue Model (Post-Pilot)

### Institution Revenue Share
- **COMET LEAPER:** 40% of per-family fees
- **Institution:** 60% of per-family fees
- **Per-family price:** $49/month (families opt-in to premium)

### Free vs. Premium
- **Free:** Basic care coordination, medication tracking, appointment reminders
- **Premium:** Advanced features, family communication tools, health insights (add $49/month if desired)

### Institutional Benefit
- Improves patient engagement and health outcomes
- Reduces care coordination burden on clinic
- Generates referral revenue if families upgrade
- Better patient retention

---

## Success Criteria (Week 4 Decision Gate)

**Green Light (Continue):**
- ✓ >60% of families active in platform
- ✓ >50% using features weekly
- ✓ >75% satisfied with tool
- ✓ Clinic sees value in care coordination benefits
- ✓ Tech integration works (manual or EMR)

**Yellow Light (Extend):**
- ~ 40-60% active, needs more time to validate
- ~ 50-75% satisfied, addressing feedback
- ~ Interest from clinic but unclear ROI yet

**Red Light (Discontinue):**
- ✗ <40% adoption or engagement
- ✗ <50% satisfied
- ✗ Clinic not willing to continue
- ✗ Technical blockers can't be resolved
