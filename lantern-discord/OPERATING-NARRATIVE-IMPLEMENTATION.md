# Operating Narrative Implementation Checklist

**Framework:** COMET LEAPER Operating Narrative v1

**Start:** Immediate

**Review:** Weekly (every Friday)

---

## Weekly Evidence Review - Monday Cadence

### Monday 9am Standup (1 hour)

**Participants:** Founder + 1-2 core team (if available)

**Agenda:**

1. **Prior week recap (15 min)**

   What experiments ran? What worked? What didn't work? Why? Update confidence on each assumption.

2. **Blocker identification (15 min)**

   Top 2-3 obstacles right now. Owner for each blocker. Timeline to resolve.

3. **This week plan (20 min)**

   3-5 priority experiments for Mon-Thu. Budget per experiment (<$200). Success metric for each. Owner assignment.

4. **Confidence update (10 min)**

   Which assumptions shifted? Update ledger with new evidence. Tie wins to measurable outcomes.

**Output:**

Weekly evidence log (share with advisors/team). Confidence ledger snapshot. Weekly priority list.

---

## Low-Burn Experiment Framework

### Daily Execution (Tue-Thu)

**Morning (30 min):**
- Select 1 experiment from weekly plan
- Define: What are we testing? What's success?
- Identify: Customers/data we need
- Budget: <$200 total spend

**Mid-day (30 min - 2 hours):**
- Run experiment (interview, feature test, message trial, etc.)
- Capture responses/outcomes in shared log
- Note surprises, insights, blockers

**End of day (15 min):**
- Quick debrief: What did we learn?
- Update confidence estimate
- Add to Friday synthesis

### Experiment Log Template

| Date | Experiment | Type | Test Assumption | Key Question | Outcome | Confidence Impact |
|------|------------|------|-----------------|---------------|---------|------------------|
| Mon | Customer interview x5 | Research | Families want weekly check-ins | Do they prefer weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly? | 4/5 prefer weekly | 60% → 85% |
| Tue | Feature test: medication reminders | Product | Reminder notifications increase adherence | Do families turn on and use reminders? | 7/10 turned on, 5/10 used weekly | 50% → 70% |

## Founder Recovery Windows

### Daily Recovery (20 minutes)
- **Morning:** 15-min walk or workout before work
- **Evening:** No email/Slack after 8pm, personal time
- **Sleep:** Target 7-8 hours (critical for decision quality)

### Weekly Recovery (4 hours)
- **Tuesday 4pm:** 60-min mentor/advisor call (support, perspective, feedback)
- **Friday 5pm:** Celebrate wins + reflect (journaling or conversation)
- **Saturday:** Full day off (family time, no work)

### Monthly Recovery (1 full day)
- **Weekend day:** No work, full personal/family focus
- **Reflection:** How's my energy? Stress level? Motivation?
- **Adjustment:** If burnout signals, reduce scope next month

**Key principle:** Founder wellbeing = company resilience

## Confidence Loops - Tied to Measurable Wins

### Month 1 Targets (Research Phase)
- **Offer validated:** 3+ institutions confirm problem
- **Revenue:** $0 (validation only)
- **Families:** 5-10 pilot participants
- **Key win:** Institutional partners understand value prop

### Month 2 Targets (Growth Phase)
- **Adoption:** 15+ family participants
- **Engagement:** >50% using platform weekly
- **Revenue:** $500-2,000 (if institutions subscribe)
- **Key win:** First institutional partner commits long-term

### Month 3 Targets (Scaling Phase)
- **Adoption:** 30+ families across 2-3 partners
- **Retention:** >80% week-to-week engagement
- **Revenue:** $2,000-5,000/month
- **Key win:** Care coordination impact measurable (health metrics improving)

### Month 4 Targets (Breakeven Approach)
- **Adoption:** 50-150+ families
- **Revenue:** $5,000-10,000/month
- **Expansion:** 3-5 institutional partners signed
- **Key win:** Unit economics validated (profitable per family)

## Friday Synthesis Meeting (90 minutes)

**3-4pm Every Friday**

**Participants:** Founder + team (if available)

**Agenda:**
1. **Experiment recap (30 min)**
   - All Mon-Thu experiments summarized
   - Learning from each (true/false/nuanced)
   - Confidence updates pooled

2. **Confidence analysis (20 min)**
   - Which assumptions shifted significantly?
   - Which need more testing?
   - Tier assumptions: High/Medium/Low priority

3. **Capital reallocation (20 min)**
   - Proven channels (70% budget): What worked?
   - Emerging opportunities (20% budget): What's promising?
   - Long shots (10% budget): Any dark horses?
   - Next week allocation based on results

4. **Next week planning (20 min)**
   - Top 3-5 experiments for Mon-Thu
   - Budget allocation
   - Owner assignment
   - Success criteria

**Output:**
- Weekly synthesis report (share with advisors)
- Next week experiment roadmap
- Updated confidence ledger
- Capital reallocation directive

---

## Monthly Synthesis (End of Month)

**Friday, Week 4 - Full day focus**

**Morning (2 hours):**
- Compile all monthly experiments
- Identify top 3 wins
- Identify top 3 blockers
- Analyze confidence drift

**Afternoon (1 hour):**
- Founder reflection: Energy? Burnout? Motivation?
- Key learnings captured
- Next month operating narrative adjusted
- Shared with advisors

**Output:**
- Monthly synthesis document
- Updated operating procedures
- Founder wellness check
