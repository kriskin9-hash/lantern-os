## What These Reports Represent

**Founder Convergence Reports** are synthesis documents that merge:
- **Evidence** from multiple repos and operational systems
- **Decision context** (what was known, what was unclear, what was decided)
- **Financial state** (cash, invoices, cleared payments, held amounts)
- **Operational narratives** (what was shipped, what failed, what's next)
- **Person-specific packets** (tailored context for team members, advisors, community)

They are institutional memory. They should not be lost or orphaned.
